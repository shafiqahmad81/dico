Key Team Members

CEO

Emillio Gallego

Co-founder of DICO and CEO since December 2020, Emilio has extensive experience in the entertainment industry. Former Globalisation Director and Unit Leader at GRE, he helped localize Japanese titles for Nintendo of Europe. Emilio focuses on building innovative teams and a positive corporate culture.

Managing Director

Matias Sanchez

Matias is an experienced executive with a background in leading operations and business management within the gaming industry. As Managing Director at DICO, he focuses on driving company growth, operational efficiency, and strategic partnerships.

Business Development & Senior PM

Ziya Sarper Ekim

Ziya is responsible for developing new business opportunities and overseeing senior project management. With extensive experience in the entertainment sector, Ziya excels in driving innovation and managing projects from ideation to completion.

Technical Artist

Alberto Guerrero Cobos

Alberto bridges the gap between art and technology. With his deep understanding of both areas, he enhances visual quality while ensuring optimal performance within the game's technical constraints.

Publishing Manager

Jose A. Gallego Valero

Jose leads the publishing team at DICO, bringing strategic and operational oversight to the successful release of client games. His expertise spans the full publishing pipeline, from production to platform launch, ensuring visibility and commercial success.

Art Director

Julio Velasco

An Emmy award-winning animator, Julio is known for his work on Lost in Oz: Extended Adventure. As Art Director at DICO, he is responsible for shaping the artistic vision of projects, ensuring creative excellence, and leading the team to achieve visually stunning outcomes.
